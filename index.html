What it does

Eight powers. Zero cameras.

Everyone else makes you choose between privacy and insight. Surds gives you both, by never looking at a face in the first place.

Privacy

Anonymous by design

It is impossible to capture personally identifiable information. Privacy is baked into the physics, not a setting.

Density

Counts, not identities

Tells one person at a desk from a team of five in a huddle, room by room, in real time.

Flow

Movement vectors

Tracks the exact vectors of movement to pinpoint high traffic bottlenecks before they form.

Activity

Dwell and hotspots

Recognises dwell times instantly, so a lounge reads as a hotspot or a dead zone at a glance.

Carbon

Auto HVAC, less carbon

Scales heating and cooling down in unoccupied zones automatically to slash the carbon footprint.

Safety

Anomaly detection

Detects changes in daily movement routines or posture anomalies, with no invasive cameras involved.

Compute

Edge or cloud

Decisions run locally on the edge, or derive deeper AI in the cloud from the same data points.

Install

Wireless in minutes

Completely wireless and battery powered, so a room goes live in minutes with no rewiring.

Most safe sensors leave you blind. Ours leaves you all-seeing, and them invisible. Density, flow and dwell, with nobody’s identity on the line.
