How an engagement runs

FIVE STEPS.
NO SURPRISES.

  1. Survey & audit

    We walk the plant, pull points, trend the loops, and write down what the building is really doing.

  2. Model in hBIM

    The building becomes a 3D twin — per-floor sectioning, isolated layers, sensors where they live.

  3. Integrate

    Every controller mapped into one namespace in Haystack or Brick. Nothing anonymous.

  4. Simulate & tune

    Setpoint changes tested on the twin before they touch a real damper.

  5. Operate

    Continuous commissioning, 24/7 fault detection, and a team that picks up the phone.
