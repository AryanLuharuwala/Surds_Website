Capabilities

One platform. Every variable that costs you energy.

Surds maps occupancy, movement, dwell, and flow in real time — and pipes that data directly into building controls. The technology is sophisticated. The result is a lower energy bill and a building that runs itself.

Energy

Auto HVAC scaling

Cuts heating and cooling in unoccupied zones automatically. 30–38% reduction in HVAC energy spend across all deployment types.

Occupancy

Room-level precision

Exact headcount per zone, updated every 340 ms. One person at a desk reads differently from a team of five — both are reported correctly.

Flow

Movement intelligence

Tracks vectors of movement across your entire floorplate. Bottlenecks and underused corridors surface before they become operational problems.

Dwell

Utilisation analytics

Identifies which zones are consistently overloaded and which are empty by default — the data your facilities team needs to reallocate space.

Compliance

Zero PII by physics

The sensor is physically incapable of capturing a face or identity. No consent flow, no GDPR liability, no data governance overhead.

Safety

Anomaly detection

Detects departure from normal movement patterns — isolated workers, sudden zone clearance, or unusual dwell in restricted areas.

Integration

BACnet · Modbus · REST

Connects to existing BMS infrastructure via standard protocols. No rip-and-replace. Surds augments what you already run.

Deployment

Live in 72 hours

Wireless, battery-powered sensors. No rewiring, no structural work, no downtime. A 50,000 sqft floor goes live in a single day.
